Our Story

A Founder's Obsession
With Your Story

Luxe Captures was born from a singular artistic vision: to capture fleeting wedding moments and immortalize them as family heirlooms. Founded in Mumbai, we've spent over two decades crafting a distinct documentary-meets-fine-art photography style.

With a collective of 20+ specialized visual artists — photographers, cinematographers, drone pilots, and editors — we operate as a single creative heartbeat. Whether capturing coastal vows in Goa, palace celebrations in Udaipur, or destination weddings across 18 countries, our lens focuses on the core of human emotion.

The Narrative

Celebration Chapters

Every wedding is a symphony of distinct events. We document each custom chapter with a dedicated visual approach.

50mm f/1.2 ◆ 1/1000s ◆ ISO 100
Chapter I

Pre-Wedding & Portraits

Thoughtfully directed, fine-art portraiture set against dramatic architectural landmarks or intimate natural landscapes. We focus on genuine interaction, movement, and soft, natural golden hour lighting to capture your relationship before the wedding celebrations begin.

Fine Art Vibe ◆ Natural Direction ◆ Scenic Scales
Chapter II

Intimate Rituals

Capturing the raw joy, colors, and emotional depth of intimate family rituals. We focus on candid expressions, the vibrant yellow splash of turmeric, detailed henna patterns, and the silent, loving glances shared between family members.

Candid Journalism ◆ Rich Color Grading ◆ Henna Details
85mm f/1.4 ◆ 1/250s ◆ ISO 200
35mm f/1.8 ◆ 1/160s ◆ ISO 1600
Chapter III

Sangeet & Revelry

The glamour, high-voltage dance choreographies, and celebration of the evening. Using specialized low-light camera configurations and fast shutter speeds, we freeze motion dynamically to deliver crisp, energetic candids of your guests dancing and celebrating.

High-Speed Shutter ◆ Low-Light Motion ◆ Glamour Lighting
Chapter IV

The Vows (Pheras)

The grand wedding ceremony in its ultimate emotional peak. We document the sacred rituals, the pheras around the holy fire, and the bidai (farewell) with documentary discretion and cinematic scale, preserving these timeless family heirlooms.

Grand Ceremony ◆ Emotional Peaks ◆ Timeless Portraits
70-200mm f/2.8 ◆ 1/400s ◆ ISO 400
