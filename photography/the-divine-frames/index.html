Filming Specifications

For clients who value raw storytelling, we utilize high-fidelity, photojournalistic setups.

Dual Prime Bodies

High-sensitivity full-frame mirrorless camera bodies configured for rapid, candid photojournalism in any ambient lighting environment.

Prime Glass Only

We bypass zoom lenses in favor of ultra-fast prime optics (f/1.2 and f/1.4), preserving high contrast and natural background separation.

Ambient Audio

Preserving localized soundboards, silent promises, and original laughter via spatial microphones to sync with your films.

Candid Masterpiece

The Bidai Farewell.

At The Divine Frames, our photojournalistic method shines in transition points. The Bidai farewell represents an intense emotional crossroad that choreographed crews often in Panchavati, Nashik often obstruct or miss.

Team operate at a distance with fast prime telephoto optics to ensure the family experiences these emotional thresholds privately.
